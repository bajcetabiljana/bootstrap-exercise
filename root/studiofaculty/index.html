1 studio

At the heart of our studio, Faculty believes communication is at its best when it is presented with clarity, honesty, and sincerity. These attributes drive the design direction at Faculty through various design mediums in both print and screen purposes. The most rewarding part of our work is being able to discover the potentials of every project and to see those possibilities come to fruition. Beyond our passion for design, we truly enjoy the relationships we have grown from working with great people and the opportunity to bring our best to further their success. Founded by Alvin Kwan and Vince Lo, Faculty is based in Vancouver, Canada.

2 team

Alvin and Vince share complementary skill sets in divergent creative thinking, art direction, and detailed execution. Both raised in Vancouver, they hope to contribute to the city through design that is both intentional and uncluttered. info@studiofaculty.com
